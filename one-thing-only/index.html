Fun Standups!

One thing only

Each team member only talks about the single most important thing they want to tell people

Take turns giving updates. Each team member only talks about the single most important thing they want to tell people.

  • Helps keep standups short and focused
  • Helps encourage team members to use other communication channels to talk about things that aren’t crucial for standup - i.e. it encourages people to actually talk to each other outside of standup
